PERSONNEL FILE: LEON SCOTT KENNEDY

The Raccoon City Incident (1998)

Leon Scott Kennedy’s trajectory from a rookie police officer to a legendary anti-bioterrorism agent is a story of grit and unparalleled survival. On September 29, 1998, Leon arrived in Raccoon City for his first day of duty with the RPD, only to find a city consumed by the T-Virus. Alongside Claire Redfield, he navigated the zombie-infested streets and uncovered the dark conspiracy involving the G-Virus and the fall of the Birkin family.

 

Evolution into a Global Operative

In the years following the Raccoon City tragedy, Leon was recruited by a secret U.S. government agency. This transition involved years of grueling combat training and psychological conditioning. This evolution was put to the ultimate test in 2004, when he was sent to a remote village in Spain to rescue the President's daughter. There, he faced the Las Plagas parasite, proving his tactical prowess against coordinated biological threats.

Tactical Profile and Legacy

Despite his many victories, Leon remains a haunted man, carrying the weight of the lives lost in Raccoon City. His stoic demeanor and expert handling of firearms make him one of the world's foremost experts on biological weaponry. As a beacon of hope in a world threatened by Umbrella's legacy, Leon continues to serve on the front lines against the dark evolution of science.
